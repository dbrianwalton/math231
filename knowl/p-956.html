Because the equation involves a quotient, we would use the quotient rule to find the related rates equation,

\begin{equation*} \frac{dE}{dt} = \frac{P \frac{dB}{dt} - B \frac{dP}{dt}}{P^2}\text{.} \end{equation*}

From the given information, we find \(P=51675\) (people) and \(\frac{dP}{dt}=-245\) (people per year) while \(B=271.3\) (million dollars) and \(\frac{dB}{dt}=-1.43\) (million dollars per year). Substituting these values into the related rates equation, we find

\begin{align*} \frac{dE}{dt} &= \frac{51675 \cdot (-1.43) - 271.3 \cdot (-245)}{(51675)^2}\\ &= -2.78 \times 10^{-6} \end{align*}

The units will be millions of dollars per person per year, so we see that the per person expense of Harrisonburg in 2020 was decreasing by $2.78 per person per year.
