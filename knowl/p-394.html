Next, the graph is concave up on \([-1,1]\text{.}\) So we find another point at \(x=1\) between the minimum at \(x=0\) and maximum at \(x=2\text{.}\) The preliminary graph is piecewise linear, decreasing on \([-1,0]\) and increasing on \([0,1]\text{.}\) We convert this to a curved arc that is concave up.
