The quadratic is already in standard form, so we can identify our coefficients \(a=3\text{,}\) \(b=-2\text{,}\) and \(c=-8\text{.}\) The product of the quadratic and constant coefficients is \(ac=-24\text{.}\) We are looking for factors of -24, \(p\) and \(q\text{,}\) so that \(pq=-24\) and \(p+q = -2\text{.}\) Thinking through these factors, we find \(p=-6\) and \(q=4\) meet this requirement.
