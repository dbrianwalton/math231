Item.

Compute the average rate of change and simplify.

\begin{align*} \frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x} &= \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\ &= \frac{\sqrt{4x+4h-1} - \sqrt{4x-1}}{h}\\ &= \frac{(\sqrt{4x+4h-1} - \sqrt{4x-1})(\sqrt{4x+4h-1} + \sqrt{4x-1})}{h(\sqrt{4x+4h-1}+\sqrt{4x-1})}\\ &= \frac{(\sqrt{4x+4h-1})^2 - (\sqrt{4x-1})^2}{h(\sqrt{4x+4h-1}+\sqrt{4x-1})}\\ &= \frac{(4x+4h-1) - (4x-1)}{h(\sqrt{4x+4h-1}+\sqrt{4x-1})}\\ &= \frac{4\cancel{h}}{\cancel{h}(\sqrt{4x+4h-1}+\sqrt{4x-1})}\\ &= \frac{4}{\sqrt{4x+4h-1}+\sqrt{4x-1}} \end{align*}
