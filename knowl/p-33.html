On the other hand, another point \((x,y)=(3,2)\) assigns different values to the variables. For this point, the expression \(3x+2y = 3(3)+2(2)=13\text{.}\) The relation's equation \(3x+2y = 12\) is false for this point. The point is not in the relation and will be off the graph.
