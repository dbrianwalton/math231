The average rate of change is the slope of the line joining these points. The units of rate of change is the ratio of the units of the corresponding variables. The average rate of change of biomass with respect to trunk diameter will therefore have units of kg/cm. The value is calculated as

\begin{equation*} \frac{\Delta M}{\Delta D} = \frac{18 \, \text{kg}}{2 \, \text{cm}} = 9 \, \text{kg/cm}\text{.} \end{equation*}
