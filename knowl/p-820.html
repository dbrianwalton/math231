When a function is differentiable on \((a,b)\text{,}\) the mean value theorem connects the average rate of change to a value of \(f'(c)\) for some \(c \in (a,b)\text{.}\) This connection allows us to use sign analysis of \(f'(x)\) to show that \(f(x)\) is increasing or decreasing on intervals.
