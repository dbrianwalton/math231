Evaluate the limit to find the derivative.

\begin{align*} f'(x) &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x}\\ &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{4}{\sqrt{4x+4h-1} + \sqrt{4x-1}}\\ &= \frac{4}{\sqrt{4x+4(0)-1} + \sqrt{4x-1}}\\ &= \frac{4}{\sqrt{4x-1} + \sqrt{4x-1}}\\ &= \frac{4}{2\sqrt{4x-1}} = \frac{2}{\sqrt{4x-1}} \end{align*}
