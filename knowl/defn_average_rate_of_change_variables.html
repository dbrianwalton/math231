Definition 1.1.1. Average Rate of Change.

For any relation between variables \(x\) and \(y\) joining two points \(A=(x_1,y_1)\) and \(B=(x_2,y_2)\text{,}\) the average rate of change of \(y\) with respect to \(x\) is defined as the ratio \(\Delta y / \Delta x\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
