The geometric interpretation of solving the inequality of the previous example is helpful. The equation \(y=3x-5\) defines a set of points (a line) in the plane. Solving \(4 \lt 3x-5 \lt 5\) is finding the \(x\)-values of the points that are between \(y=4\) and \(y=5\text{.}\)
